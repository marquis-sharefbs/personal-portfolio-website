Portfolio Website Project

Essential pages/sections:

Hero section — your name, a one-line bio, and a call to action (e.g. "View my work" or "Contact me")
About — 2–3 sentences about you, your background, and what you're looking for, include up-to-date action photo
Projects — even 1–2 is fine to start; name, screenshot or demo, short description, and a GitHub link
Skills — a simple list of languages/tools you know
Contact — your email and links to GitHub and LinkedIn

Nice to have:

Responsive design (looks good on mobile)
A downloadable resume link
Smooth scrolling between sections

Keep it simple — one page with anchor links is totally fine. Clean and functional beats fancy and broken every time.
